Discover the Rich History of Ethiopia

Ethiopia is home to some of the most significant and ancient historical sites in the world. From the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela to the Bale Mountains National Park, explore the beauty and history of this incredible country.

Featured Sites

Lalibela Rock-Hewn Churches

Discover the ancient, monolithic rock-hewn churches carved in the 12th century.

Axum Obelisks

Visit the towering obelisks in the ancient city of Axum, once the capital of the Axumite Empire.

Fasil Ghebbi (Gondar)

Explore the fortress-city in Gondar, home to the 17th-century palaces and historic structures.

Lake Tana Monasteries

Visit the ancient monasteries surrounding Lake Tana, the source of the Blue Nile.

Tiya Megaliths

Konso Cultural Landscape

Explore the unique terraced agricultural system and traditional villages of the Konso people.

Bale Mountains National Park

Home to diverse ecosystems and rare wildlife, including the Ethiopian wolf and endemic birds.
